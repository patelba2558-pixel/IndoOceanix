Our Specialty

Pure Sugarcane Jaggery

Known as 'Golden Sugar,' our jaggery is 100% organic, chemical-free, and rich in minerals. We offer various forms to suit industrial and retail needs.

  • Jaggery Blocks: Traditional solid cubes, perfect for long-term storage and bulk use.
  • Jaggery Powder: Easy-to-dissolve, healthy alternative to refined white sugar.
  • Liquid Jaggery (Kakvi): A nutritious syrup used in gourmet cooking and health tonics.
